# Product Marketing Context

## Product
**peptidejackpotletsgetrich** — positioned as "NerdWallet for peptides."

Four things in one:
1. Peptide research platform (education, guides, compound pages)
2. Cycle tracking + recommendation SaaS (planner/tracker, monthly subscription)
3. Affiliate platform (outbound to RUO vendors — Amino Club, XL Peptides, etc.)
4. Paid plans — fitness plans for peptides, diet plans for peptides

## Audience
Research-curious consumers evaluating peptides. Split across first-time researchers, experienced stackers, and fitness/longevity-oriented buyers.

## Regulatory Framing
Research peptides are compliance-sensitive. Site uses "research use only" (RUO) framing consistently. Meta/Google paid ads will be rejected for peptide products — channel strategy must route around paid social/search.
